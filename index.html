Cats are obligated carnivores and love meat-based foods like chicken, turkey, fish, and beef. Wet food, especially with a meaty sauce or pate, is often their favorite due to its texture and flavor. Many cats enjoy fish, such as tuna or salmon, and some also like high-quality dry food. Treats like catnip or small pieces of cooked meat can also be a hit, appealing to their natural hunting instincts.

Cats are playful and curious animals. They love exploring their surroundings, chasing toys, and pouncing on moving objects like laser pointers. Napping in sunny spots is a favorite activity, along with grooming to keep their fur clean. Cats also enjoy scratching to stretch and mark their territory. Whether playing, resting, or observing, they find joy in exploring their environment.
